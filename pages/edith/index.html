Edith

the Ancient Guard

The stone man who signed a contract with the Ancient Ones has a kind-hearted nature and infinite power. He sleeps most of his life. After Grock woke up, he found that the Ancient Ones he was guarding had disappeared, so he carried the Castle of the Ancient Ones on his shoulders and walked out of the mountains to find the whereabouts of the Ancient Ones.

Then she once again helped her foster father enter the temple to search for scraps, the guardian of the ruins-Elemental Giant Uranus was prepared to destroy the invaders. It was then that Edith remembered everything, but her humanity overpowered her divine nature, and she fought Uranus to protect her foster father. Just then, the Celestial Palace fell and the sealed Evil Gods scattered. Edith parted with her foster father, drove her Mecha "Phylax" and set out on a journey to hunt down fleeing Evil Gods.
